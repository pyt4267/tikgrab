1. Introduction

TikGrab ("the Service") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

2.2 Information You Provide

  • TikTok video URLs (entered for download)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving our services

4. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience and enhance our services. Cookies are used for:

  • Saving user preferences (e.g., theme settings)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

5. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

6. Advertising

We use third-party advertising services (Google AdSense) on our Service. These advertisers may use cookies to display ads based on your interests.

For more information about Google AdSense, please see Google's Advertising Policies.

7. Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information. However, please understand that no transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary. We will notify you of significant changes on our website.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about privacy, please contact us through our Contact Page.
